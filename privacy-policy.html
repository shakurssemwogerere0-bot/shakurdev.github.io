Privacy Policy for Currency Pro

Effective date: 19 April 2026

Currency Pro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

1. Information We Collect

Currency Pro may use third-party services that can collect certain information automatically, such as device information, app usage information, IP address, and advertising identifiers.

Currency Pro itself does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number, unless otherwise stated in the app.

2. How the App Works

Currency Pro uses an internet connection to fetch live currency exchange rates. Some data may be processed by third-party service providers to enable core app features.

3. Advertising

Currency Pro may display ads provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect and process limited device and usage data to provide, improve, and measure ads.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including Google AdMob and an exchange rate API provider. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

Currency Pro does not sell users’ personal information. Data handled by third-party SDKs or service providers is governed by their own privacy practices.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app functionality and user information, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Currency Pro is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
