# Privacy Policy – Currency Pro

Last updated: April 2026

Currency Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

Currency Pro does not collect, store, or require any personal information such as your name, email address, or login credentials.

However, the app may automatically collect certain non-personal information through third-party services, including:

* Device information (device type, operating system, version)
* Anonymous usage data
* Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
* IP address (used for network communication)

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

* Provide accurate currency conversion services
* Fetch real-time exchange rates from external APIs
* Display advertisements within the app
* Improve app performance and user experience

## 3. Third-Party Services

Currency Pro uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you indirectly:

### Google AdMob

Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized and non-personalized ads.

Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Currency API Providers

Used to fetch live exchange rates. These services may process network-related data such as IP address but do not collect personal user data.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Currency Pro does not store any personal data on its servers.

Any cached data (such as last exchange rates) is stored locally on your device and is not shared externally.

We take reasonable measures to protect app integrity and ensure safe data handling.

## 5. Offline Usage

Currency Pro may function in offline mode using previously cached exchange rates. No additional data is collected during offline use.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Currency Pro is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

## 7. User Control

Users can:

* Use the app without creating an account
* Disable internet access (which disables live updates)
* Reset app data via device settings

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## 9. Consent

By using Currency Pro, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
